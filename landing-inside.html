Why SlipUp Inside?

SlipUp Inside is built for teams, units, and groups. Log moments when they happen. Share what you choose. See heat, shift, and support — all anonymous.

Three kinds of moments

  • Heat – Tension, conflict, close call. Notice what raised it.
  • Shift – Different choice than usual. Stretches, experiments.
  • Support – Help you gave, got, or saw. Learn without the pain.

How it works

Log moments – One line when something happens. Heat, shift, or support.

Share to group – Select which moments to share. Others see anonymous counts and patterns — no names.

Reflect – End-of-day reflection: heat to cool down, shifts you're glad for.

Intentions – Set a daily focus. See what others in your group are focusing on.
