What SlipUp does for you

SlipUp is a simple tracker that turns “I messed up again” into a concrete signal you can work with.

1. Daily mental clarity

See where your day actually goes wrong: repeated triggers, fragile moments, and patterns you can’t spot in your head alone.

2. A personal learning system

Every entry is tagged into a small mental model you can act on:

  • Avoidable → slips you want less of
  • Fertile → experiments and useful failures
  • Observed → things you notice in others or systems

3. A gentle push to reflect

At the end of the day you get a short reflection prompt and an “exploration index” — how much of your error was fertile vs. avoidable.

How to use it (30 seconds)

  • During the day: whenever you slip, add a quick note (or just +1 with no text).
  • Choose the type: avoidable, fertile, or observed.
  • In the evening: glance at your counts and write one line about what to change or keep.

You don’t need an account. Everything is saved locally on your device by default.

What you’ll see inside

  • Today / week / month views so you can zoom out from one bad day.
  • Exploration index showing the balance between avoidable and fertile mistakes.
  • Recent log of your last entries so you remember what actually happened.
  • Optional reflection box to write a single line on what you’re learning.
