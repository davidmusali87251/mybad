What you’re really selling

Not statistics, counters, or charts. You’re selling:

1. Daily mental clarity

A place to see patterns, reduce anxiety about making mistakes, and turn failures into information. That’s cognitive hygiene.

2. A personal learning system

Three categories that form a mental model — a small philosophical system turned into a tool.

  • Avoidable → discipline
  • Fertile → growth
  • Observed → systemic awareness

3. Healthy error culture

Anonymous entries from all users. A database of real human errors that can become reports, trends, behavioral insights, and organizational culture.

Strategic advantage

Most apps are about productivity, habits, goals, tasks. Mistake Tracker is about awareness, error, learning, and reflection — a much less saturated territory.
