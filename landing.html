Why SlipUp?

SlipUp is a calm place to park mistakes so they stop spinning in your head and start turning into learning.

It’s like having a mirror instead of a critic. I see patterns I’d never noticed.
Two minutes at night. My head feels clearer.

Three simple types

  • Avoidable – “This keeps happening.” You caught a repeat or autopilot slip. Note it, see the pattern, then tweak. Goal: fewer repeats over time.
  • Fertile – “I stretched. I tried something new.” Experiment, stretch, fail forward. Note it so you remember what worked and what to try next. Goal: keep the experiments that grow you.
  • Observed – “I noticed this in others or the system.” Not yours, but useful. Learn without the sting. Goal: borrow wisdom and apply it before it becomes yours.

Roadmap

Now – Personal: Note by type (avoidable, fertile, observed), see your stats, and reflect in one line: what to reduce, what to keep. Your data stays on your device.

Next – Social: Share your numbers anonymously. See how your patterns compare with others. No names, no pressure—just a mirror for the world.

Later – Teams: SlipUp Inside for groups and facilitators. Heat, Shift, Support views so teams learn together without exposing individual entries.
