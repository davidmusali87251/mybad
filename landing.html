Why SlipUp?

SlipUp is a calm place to park mistakes so they stop spinning in your head and start turning into learning.

Three simple types

  • Avoidable – “I knew better.” Reduce repeats.
  • Fertile – “I tried something new.” Keep the experiments.
  • Observed – “I saw this in others or the system.” Learn without the pain.

Roadmap

Now – Personal: Log mistakes by type, see simple stats, and add a short daily reflection.

Next – Social: Stronger anonymous sharing and summaries so you can compare your exploration with others.

Later – Teams: Group views (SlipUp Inside) for facilitators and teams, without exposing individual entries.
